Title: MBC (Main Blood Center) - Healthcare Digital Ecosystem
Style: Professional, Modern, Trustworthy, and Clean.
Color Palette: Medical White, Deep Emergency Red (#D32F2F), and Soft Gray for backgrounds.

Core Concept:
Build a high-performance healthcare platform named "MBC" that connects blood donors, patients, and hospitals in a seamless real-time network. The design should be "Mobile-First" since emergency users will access it via smartphones. Use clear typography (Sans Serif) and high-quality medical iconography.

The 8-Page Structure & Content:

Home Page (Landing):

Hero Section: A powerful headline: "Every Drop Counts. Connect. Donate. Save Lives."

CTA Buttons: "Request Blood Now (Emergency)" and "Register as a Donor."

Stats Counter: Live numbers of successful donations and registered hospitals.

Donor Registration Page:

A multi-step form (Personal Info -> Blood Type -> Last Donation Date -> Location).

Eligibility checklist (Interactive icons for health requirements).

Blood Request Page:

Urgent request form for patients/hospitals.

Fields: Blood type required, number of units, hospital location, and urgency level.

Hospital & Center Locator:

Interactive Map integration showing nearby hospitals and blood banks.

Filter by: City, Availability of specific blood types.

Educational Hub (The Science):

Information about blood types (Compatibility Chart).

Benefits of donation and post-donation care tips.

Emergency Dashboard (Live Feed):

A real-time "Ticker" or list showing active urgent requests in the user's area.

About MBC & Partners:

The mission of the "Main Blood Center."

Logos of partner hospitals and health organizations to build trust.

Contact & Support:

Emergency hotline, FAQ section, and a feedback form for users.

Technical Requirements:

Clean navigation bar with a highlighted "Donate" button.

Fast loading speed and SEO-optimized structure.

Accessibility features (High contrast for readability).

نصائح إضافية لضمان نجاح الموقع:
التدرج اللوني: استخدم اللون الأحمر بحذر (للتنبيهات والأزرار المهمة فقط) حتى لا يسبب القلق للمستخدم؛ اجعل اللون الأبيض هو السائد ليعطي إحساساً بالنظافة والاحترافية الطبية.

خرائط تفاعلية: إذا كان الموقع يدعم البرمجة، تأكد من ربط صفحة "المستشفيات" بـ Google Maps API.

سهولة الوصول: في حالات الطوارئ، الشخص يكون متوتراً، لذا اجعل زر "طلب دم" هو الأبرز والأسهل في الوصول إليه (غالباً في أعلى اليمين أو منتصف الشاشة).
